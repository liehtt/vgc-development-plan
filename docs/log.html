📊 My log

Personal game journal for the training plan. Everything lives in your browser — never leaves your device.

🔒 Your log lives only in your browser

This site has no server. Everything you record is stored in your browser's localStorage and never leaves your device. Other visitors to this site have their own separate logs in their own browsers — your data is invisible to them.

If you clear your browser data, switch to incognito mode, or use a different device, your log doesn't follow you automatically. Download a backup at least weekly using the button on the dashboard.

🎯 Phase 2 drills

Phase 2 of the training plan is the anticipation layer — building reference data (speed map, benchmarks) and drill habits (prediction, replay-watching) that fix the "I default into my familiar script" plateau.

Reading + checkpoint are housekeeping. Prediction + replay drills are the work. Speed map + benchmarks live under Current team since they're team-reference data.

Phase 2 reading list. Mark each off as you finish. Optional notes for what stuck.
    🎯 Mid-game prediction drill 0 sessions
    Phase 2 hard test. Per turn, predict the opp's move + expected damage range. After the turn: was the prediction right? Pass criterion: ≥3 of 5 ladder games where you correctly predicted at least one turn.
    📺 Replay drill log 0 drills
    Weekly drill. Pick a pro replay, pause before each turn, write what you'd do, then watch + listen to the caster. 3 turns of one replay per session is plenty (~15 min).
    ✅ Phase 2 checkpoint 0 / 6 verified
    Self-attestation gate to Phase 3. Mark "passed" only when you can demonstrate the skill — evidence textarea is required. You need 4 of 6 to advance to Phase 3.
